Dear Global Shop Solutions,

I believe I should be selected as a recipient of one of these awards because I am passionate about this field and not afraid of adversity. I am eager to contribute to a common goal, as I value teamwork and believe in the process of growth. Throughout my time as a MIS major, I have worked on various team projects, including creating a Microsoft Access database, coding a website for a Thai massage company using HTML and CSS, and building the backend database using SQL. From these projects, I have not only strengthened my technical abilities but also seen firsthand what problem-solving technical issues looks like in a group setting.

I chose this major because it checks off the boxes of the interests I have had since I was a child. I grew up building computers, saving up my money to buy new components, and checking “PCPartPicker” for deals after school. I have always been interested in computers, so when it came time to pick a major, MIS was a no-brainer. With this degree, I want to grow my technical and personal skills in IT, working with like-minded individuals to solve technical problems using a medium I have been fascinated with since I was a kid.

I made the right decision by choosing MIS, and with the support of fellow students and faculty, such as Dr. K, I believe my aspirations in the field of IT will come to fruition, and being a recipient of this award would be the embodiment of that.

Sincerely,
